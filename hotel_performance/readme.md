**Business problem:**  
The management of a hotel chain needed a unified view of key metrics (occupancy, average check, revenue per room) for 3★ properties, as well as competitive positioning for 4★ hotels.

**My contribution:**  
I combined 2023–2024 data, calculated ADR, RevPAR, and occupancy, and visualised competitive benchmarks to highlight the chain’s strengths.
