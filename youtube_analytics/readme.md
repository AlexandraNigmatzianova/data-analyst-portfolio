## 🎥 Project 3: YouTube Category Popularity by Country (Tableau)

**Business problem:**  
Determine which content type (Music, Entertainment, Gaming, Blogs, etc.) is most popular in different countries – useful for marketers and content creators planning expansion.

**My contribution:**  
I took a Kaggle dataset (2023), cleaned it (removed rows with missing values), and built an interactive world map in Tableau.

### 🔗 Interactive Dashboard (Tableau Public)
👉 **[View the live dashboard (clickable)](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/aleksandra.nigmatzianova/viz/YoutubeCategoryPopularityByCountry/Sheet1)**

### 📌 Key Findings:

1. **Music is the absolute leader** in most countries:  
   *Canada, USA, Brazil, Colombia, Argentina, India, Iraq, Turkey, Sweden, UK, Morocco, Netherlands.*
2. **Entertainment** dominates in:  
   *Japan, Germany, Italy, Ukraine, Jordan, Indonesia.*
3. **Blogs & Gaming** also rank in the top 4 across many countries.  
4. **Niche leaders** (interesting exceptions):  
   - **Sports** leads only in **Switzerland**.  
   - **Politics & News** – an unexpected leader in **Bangladesh**.  
   - **Comedy** is most popular in **Latvia**.  
   - **Film & Animation** is the top choice in **Malaysia**.
