## 📈 Project 2: Consumer Price Dynamics (Power BI)

**Business problem:**  
To analyse multi‑year price trends across a wide basket of goods (from utilities and food to medicine) in order to identify seasonality and inflation patterns.

**My contribution:**  
I processed a public dataset, set up filters for product categories, and visualised long‑term trends so users can compare dynamics for gas, beef, clothing, and pharmaceuticals on a single page.

> **Insight:** The chart instantly reveals which product categories have been inflating fastest over recent years and which remain stable.
